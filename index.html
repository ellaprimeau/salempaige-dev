THE THIRD SELF

In the captivating realm of poetry, we find ourselves immersed in a conversation with Salem Paige, a remarkable transgender poet whose introspective works have captivated readers worldwide. With their latest book, "The Third Self," Paige takes us on a profound journey of self-discovery and creative exploration.

Residing on Algonquin Anishinaabe territory, Salem Paige graciously invites us into their creative universe. Their poetic voice, filled with sublime imagery and existential introspection, sets the stage for a conversation that promises to be both enlightening and transformative.
